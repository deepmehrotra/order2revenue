Return Manager

Sale Returns is an inherent part of E-Seller's everyday Routine.According to the latest data,an average of 10% of the sales are being returned in real world.Lucrative and flexible Returns Policies enables a customer to return products post 30 days from delivery in some cases.Tracking returns is indeed cumbersome.

Manage your Returns/RTO’s in the most efficient way possible by leveraging the universal search feature of O2R.Search with almost any detail available,be it the shipment number or the customer information.When receiving a Sale Return,O2R also advises if the partner specific Return limit has been crossed or not and account for the same accordingly.

TAX/TDS Manager

Effective TAX and TDS reporting, filing,reimbursing is an inevitable task for any business owner.While this can be plain sailing for a man of other trade, for an E-Seller its convoluted.The reason for the same can be understood given the complex nature of various sales channels commissions, high volume of transactions and sale returns. To add to the concern, TAX/TDS dues are not accounted for a static data but an everchanging data. Filling and Depositing TDS/TAX is not a one time job. The same has to be chronically performed month after month before its due dates. Failure to do so is a legal offense.

O2R manages an E-sellers TAX/TDS dues effectively in real time and reports the same to the seller.O2R not only addresses every concern stated above, but more importantly it calculates TAX/TDS dues based on seller data instead of Sales Channel Data, thus providing answer for every invoice that has been generated.O2R provides timely alerts for depositing TAX/TDS dues.

N/R and Return Charges Calculator

O2R is a semi-automated accounting tool for e-sellers which intakes limited inputs from sellers,only required to guarantee accurate and trustworthy results. The seller needs to tell O2R, how much was the product sold for,why was the product returned and O2R derives what is to be received,and how much return charges will be deducted against that order,based on the predefined terms and conditions agreed with sales channels. O2R is the ultimate solution as it does not show what the Sales channel wish to show but instead independently calculates the Net Receivable(N/R) and Return Charges against an Order based on seller inputs. Some of other accounting solutions for E-sellers may promise completely automated solutions but what is the point of paying for a software which only tells you what sales channels want to convey.If Sales Channels provided data is to believed,then why use any accounting software at all.

Events

Events are an everyday part of an E-seller’s business.On an Average, every Sales Channel hosts at least one major promotion in a month. Given the complicated commission structures it becomes cumbersome to identify and account for orders differently than the regular agreed terms and conditions for all channels separately.With O2R, the sellers can easily schedule or configure events without affecting their regular configurations.The seller can also easily identify Event Orders from the regular orders.

Upcoming and Outstanding Payments

In the midst of this complicated and dynamic business atmosphere,E-sellers completely rely on assumptions and have absolutely no clarity on their outstanding and upcoming payments. O2R gives a very clear picture on this subject in real time. With O2R the seller can know his outstanding payments (that have crossed their expected payment dates) and also the upcoming payments (that have been shipped already and are yet to reach their respective payment dates.
